Founded in 1994 by Mike Meldman, Discovery Land Company is a US-based real estate developer and operator of private residential communities and resorts with a world-renowned portfolio of domestic and international properties.

Our Intention

We create unique, members-only settings where families can create intergenerational memories and enrich their lives amongst nature’s exquisite beauty. Everything we do is driven by our desire to create one-of-a-kind spaces where families can be together.

The Discovery Difference

The Discovery Story

For almost three decades, Discovery has built communities that encourage both our development teams and members to dream up and create the most fun experiences imaginable. The first development in Scottsdale set the standard for a Discovery community, complete with a championship golf course, comfort stations brimming with snacks, luxurious residential offerings, five-star service, and more. Since then, each community has built on the fun from the one that came before.
